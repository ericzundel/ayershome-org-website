# About Me

Hi, I'm **Eric Ayers** from Atlanta, Georgia, USA.

Welcome to my personal website! This is where I share my thoughts, projects, and experiences.

## Background

I'm a native of the Atlanta metro area, growing up in Hapeville, East Point, and Fairburn. I'm a Georgia Tech graduate and have lived all around the city over the years.

Although I primarily identify myself as a professional Software Developer, I've worn many hats throughout my career. My work history started as a teenager and includes:

- Food vendor at the Georgia Renaissance Festival
- Auto Parts Store stockboy
- Paint Salesman at Sears
- Intern at an Architectural Firm
- Undergrad Research Assistant (multimedia, physics, GTRI)
- Teaching Assistant
- Software Developer
- Engineering Manager
- Makerspace Manager
- Startup Co-Founder

This diverse background has given me a broad perspective on problem-solving and working with people from all walks of life.

## Skills & Interests

- Open source contributions

- Robotics and making technology accessible
- Education and teaching
- Building and creating
- Getting around on my [Edison electric bicycle](https://edisonbicycles.com/)
