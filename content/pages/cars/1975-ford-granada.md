## Eric-o-meter: 4/10

What I remember most about this car was that the doors were long and heavy. It was so similar to the Monarch, I got the two of them confused. My mother used to have a plastic cupholder that sat on the transmission tunnel. The car smelled heavily of spilled coffee.
